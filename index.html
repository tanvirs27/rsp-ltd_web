Mission and Vision


Charcoal Powder:
The Jute Stick Charcoal Powder is made from Jute Stick. After high temperature anaerobic charring we get the Jute Stick Charcoal Powder. This kind of Charcoal Powder is in wide range of uses viz. environmental protection, water and other liquid purification, building heat preservation medicine manufacturing and fireworks and so on. Without adding any additives, chemicals or bonding agents, our charcoal powder is completely natural and odor free. By reducing waste discharge into environment, rather by making full use of resources and technology, we make Charcoal Powder totally environment friendly and make the world a better place to live. The technology and the skilled man power in making charcoal powder are available in the country. Our target of sales is around 3000 MT of Charcoal Powder per year.

Jute Products:
We have plan to grip China, Japan, Turkey, United Stages, Australia, Taiwan, Canada, Maxico, Republic Korea and Europe etc market by exporting jute stick products.
