RSP BD Limited

It is a Joint Venture of Bangladesh and China owned purely privately by Bangladeshi and Chinese Entrepreneurs viz. Md Akram Ali Khan and Ms. Ju juan Zeng. We aspire to contribute to the earnings of valuable foreign exchange for Bangladesh by exporting ‘Jute Stick Charcoal’ abroad. The product is produced by using our very natural and local Jute Sticks at our Factory at Akdanta, Atgharia, Pabna.

Mission and Vision

Charcoal Powder:
The Jute Stick Charcoal Powder is made from Jute Stick. After high temperature anaerobic charring we get the Jute Stick Charcoal Powder. This kind of Charcoal Powder is in wide range of uses viz. environmental protection, water and other liquid purification, building heat preservation medicine manufacturing and fireworks and so on. Without adding any additives, chemicals or bonding agents, our charcoal powder is completely natural and odor free. By reducing waste discharge into environment, rather by making full use of resources and technology, we make Charcoal Powder totally environment friendly and make the world a better place to live. The technology and the skilled man power in making charcoal powder are available in the country. Our target of sales is around 3000 MT of Charcoal Powder per year.
Jute Products:
We want to make the environment of the world freer of adulteration and other pollution. Having a thought for the Green Project we have a plan to manufacture environment friendly items for people’s use. We have our own jute cultivating field where we can grow jute for our factory. We use trained and expert people for de-skinning of jute from raw jute, making maximum extraction and best output. We will have most of our offices run by solar power saving power from organic fuels. In addition we will have our Bio- Gas plant for energy for the factory saving natural gas (Titas Gas etc) of the country. Our aim is also to go for manufacturing Jute Products viz. Jute Bags, Hessian Cloth, Canvas, Sacking Cloths, shopping bags and jute fabrics etc by establishing another Factory in the same vicinity. We shall go for production by year 2018 which will create job opportunity for a good no. of people of the country. In addition the Jute cultivators may be benefitted by selling their products to us. The capacity of the factory will be around 40 MT per day. The entire product is likely to be exported as it is in high demand all over the world.
